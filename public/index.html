What is FlowForge?

FlowForge is a platform designed to enhance Node-RED workflows with collaborative features, security, and scalability for developers. Whether it's automation, IoT, or real-time data, FlowForge provides the tools to optimize deployment and manage workflows seamlessly.

Key Features

Collaborative Development

Enable multiple developers to work on the same instance, speeding up development and reducing conflicts.

Remote Deployment

Deploy your Node-RED flows across servers and edge devices seamlessly with minimal setup.

Snapshot Management

Take automatic snapshots of your deployments and roll back to previous states whenever necessary.

DevOps Ready

Integrate with your DevOps pipelines for continuous delivery and integration.

Security Built-In

FlowForge ensures that your workflow and data are secure with robust access control and monitoring.

Use Cases

Leverage FlowForge to integrate data from patient monitoring devices, automating workflow and data collection.

Real-time monitoring of production lines with predictive maintenance to reduce downtime and enhance productivity.

Automate financial data collection and reporting workflows securely, ensuring compliance and security.
