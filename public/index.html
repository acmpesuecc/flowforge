What is FlowForge?

FlowForge is an open-source platform built on top of Node-RED to help teams collaborate, manage, and deploy data workflows in a secure and scalable environment. It enhances the capabilities of Node-RED with remote deployment, collaboration tools, and DevOps support, making it ideal for businesses across industries.

Key Features

Collaborative Development

Work with multiple developers on the same Node-RED instance, enabling faster development and teamwork.

Remote Deployment

Deploy your Node-RED workflows across servers and edge devices with ease and automation.

Snapshot Management

Keep your deployments safe with automated snapshots for reliable rollbacks and restorations.

DevOps Integration

Integrate FlowForge with your CI/CD pipeline for continuous development and deployment of Node-RED applications.

Enhanced Security

Maintain a secure environment for your development and deployment processes with built-in security features.

Installation

FlowForge supports Linux, macOS, and Windows. It requires Node.js v16 and includes installation scripts for hassle-free setup. Follow the complete guide to get FlowForge running on your system.
